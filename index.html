How to play Bartending Quiz!

  • Firstly you will be asked to enter a username.Choose any name that you desire and secure your spot on the leader board
  • Press Play! and a timer will start.
  • You have 50 seconds to choose which of the 3 given options is the right answer to the question given.
  • Choose your answer by clicking on it!
